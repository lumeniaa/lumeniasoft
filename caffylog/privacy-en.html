Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Important: Caffylog respects your privacy and does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All your data is stored locally on your device.

1. Overview

This privacy policy explains how the Caffylog mobile application ("App") works and how it protects your data. At LumeniaSoft, we consider protecting our users' privacy as our top priority.

2. Information Collected

2.1 Personal Information

Caffylog does not collect any personal information. The app does not require:

  • Name, surname
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location information
  • Camera or microphone access

2.2 App Usage Data

The app stores the following data locally on your device only for functionality:

  • Coffee consumption records (type, size, time)
  • Sleep records (duration, quality score)
  • Personal limit settings
  • Language preference

This data is never sent to servers or shared with third parties.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored in your device's local storage (SQLite database). This data:

  • Exists only on your device
  • Does not require internet connection
  • Is not backed up to cloud servers
  • Is completely removed when you uninstall the app

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect:

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • Advertising identifiers
  • IP address

For AdMob's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Ad Personalization

To disable personalized ads:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

5. Data Security

Since your data is stored locally on your device:

  • There is no risk of data breach
  • Not affected by server attacks
  • Does not require encryption (local storage)

6. Children's Privacy

The app is designed for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  • In-app: Settings → Reset All Data
  • Device settings: Uninstall the app (all data will be deleted)

8. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about our privacy policy:

  • Email: caffylogapp@lumeniasoft.com
  • Web: lumeniasoft.com
Summary: Caffylog respects your privacy. It does not collect any personal data, all data stays on your device, and you can delete it anytime.
